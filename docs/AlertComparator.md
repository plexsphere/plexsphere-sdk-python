# AlertComparator

Crossing direction an alert rule records the signal against the threshold with: `gt` strictly above, `gte` at or above, `lt` strictly below, `lte` at or below. 

## Enum

* `AlertComparatorGreaterThan` (value: `'gt'`)

* `AlertComparatorGreaterOrEqual` (value: `'gte'`)

* `AlertComparatorLessThan` (value: `'lt'`)

* `AlertComparatorLessOrEqual` (value: `'lte'`)

# BackupPriority

Disaster-recovery restore priority of a backed-up store: `P0` is the most critical (restore first), `P3` the least critical (gaps self-heal after restore). 

## Enum

* `BackupPriorityP0` (value: `'P0'`)

* `BackupPriorityP1` (value: `'P1'`)

* `BackupPriorityP2` (value: `'P2'`)

* `BackupPriorityP3` (value: `'P3'`)
